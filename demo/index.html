ACT Upload (Access Control)

Upload content with Access Control Trie. Only grantees (and the publisher) can decrypt.

💡 Get Bee node's public key: curl -s http://localhost:1633/addresses | jq -r '.publicKey'

ACT Download (Self-Decrypt)

Download and decrypt ACT content using the publisher's identity (via proxy).

ACT Add Grantees

Add new grantees to an existing ACT. Use this to grant access after initial upload.

SOC (Single Owner Chunk)

Upload and download Single Owner Chunks. Encrypted upload returns an encryption key required for download.
